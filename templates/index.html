DEFENCE HOUSING
AUTHORITY

Defence Housing Authority (DHA) is a Government of Pakistan owned real-estate development company that governs housing and municipal services for Defence neighbourhoods across Pakistan.

Who We Are

With a bona fide inception, Pakistan Defence Officers Housing Authority was established for the welfare of honourable officers of the Armed Forces of Pakistan. The prime aim was to provide exceptional standards of life to the officers who have been serving the state for years. The Housing Society began with an allotment of mere 76.2 acres of land but through remarkable planning and meticulous urban development, DHA evolved as the largest and leading residential estate of the country.

Our Services

SECURITY

DHA has a well-established security system to protect and safeguard its people and assets. Security crew of DHA is vigilant and able to deter illegal and inappropriate actions either directly being stationed on duty places, through patrols, or indirectly, by monitoring alarm systems or video surveillance cameras.

EDUCATION

DHAES aims to provide a high standard of education in a warm and friendly environment focusing on personality development of students with emphasis on character building and grooming to be an asset to the society.

MEDICAL FACILITIES

DHA Medical Centre is one of the best healthcare service providers in Pakistan. Providing you with the best patient experience and medical professionals. Treatments and Diagnostic services at DHA Medical Center are carried out through latest healthcare technology.

MAINTENANCE

Repairs & maintenance is one of the most important components of the services provided by a residential society to its residents. Not only is it the service that affects the residents alone but also is most highly valued having impact quality of life. In recent years there have been massive changes to the way traditional repairs and maintenance services are delivered. The focus is on proactive approach towards providing continuous maintenance support in order to ensuring quality living.

RELIGIOUS AFFAIRS

DHA promotes Islamic values, teachings and religious harmony. In DHA “ALL MOSQUES ARE FOR ALL MUSLIMS”, no Mosque in DHA is specified for any particular school of thought, sect or religious group. DHA gives equal importance to the Religious Affairs of all residents.

CLUBS

Defence Housing Authority Lahore believes it is essential to provide requisite facilities to its residents which are mandatory in an urban society in accordance with our social and cultural values. The incitation of establishing Defence Clubs was a seed planted years ago which now has become a lush green tree.
